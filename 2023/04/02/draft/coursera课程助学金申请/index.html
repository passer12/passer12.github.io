助学金申请内容

coursera是一个相当知名的慕课网站，但是有些课程的证书或者测验需要付费，它很人性化地提供了学生助学金计划，基本申请都能过。这是我的申请内容模板。

My goal is to become an expert in the field of machine learning so that I can solve more and more challenging questions in this field and make a valuable contribution to society. Additionally, I hope to repay my parents for their hard work in supporting my education and life.

This course will help me gain a deeper understanding of advanced learning algorithms, which will play a crucial role in the field of machine learning. It is important to have a solid foundation in order to succeed and thrive in any field, and I believe that this course will provide me with the necessary knowledge and skills to solve increasingly complex problems in machine learning.

Through this effort, I hope to achieve my dream of becoming a highly skilled machine learning expert and improve the quality of life for both myself and my family. I am committed to working hard towards this goal and thank you for your support.
